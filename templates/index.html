The Basics

On different celestial bodies, the effect of weight is different. A planet with a stronger gravitational pull will have give the effect of being heavier, while a planet with a weaker gravitational pull will give the effect of feeling lighter. Try going to one of the different pages to find out what your weight would be on some of our Solar System's most popular celestial bodies.
